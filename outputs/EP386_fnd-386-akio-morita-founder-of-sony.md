## Background

> Senra rereads Made in Japan (1986) after We Were Burning and founder cross-references from Bezos and Jobs. Morita, 25, gathered in a war-devastated department store; Hiroshima news struck him as proof of overwhelming American industry — Ford River Rouge at 2,000 acres terrified him. A 300-year sake heir who flunked school for electronics, he partnered with Ibuka in Telecommunications Research Laboratories, won U.S. military contracts with sand-bucket fire safety, and fought Regency to establish transistor pocket radios and Walkman culture.
