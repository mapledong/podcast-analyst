## Key Insights

> **1.** Channel is the product in FMCG.
>
> **Q** What unifies the three companies?
>
> **A** Wen Yongsheng argues winning distribution beats ad — Nongfu freezers, Midea dealers, Anta stores are moat assets investors must KPI separately per ticker.

> **2.** Dealer profit sharing sustains loyalty.
>
> **Q** Why do networks not defect?
>
> **A** Each model shares margin so dealers push SKU — when rebates compress, watch inventory and sell-through before revenue headlines.

> **3.** Three tickers, three channel physics.
>
> **Q** Should investors basket them?
>
> **A** No — 9633.HK is beverage placement, 000333.SZ is appliance supply chain global, 2020.HK is sports brand retail. Same lesson, different unit economics and cyclicality.
