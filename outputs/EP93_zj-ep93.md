## Key Insights

> **1.** Feeds already are misaligned AGI.
>
> **Q** Why does a product guy warn about AGI?
>
> **A** Zhang Qianchuan built systems that maximize dwell time — that is powerful optimization without explicit human values. Scaling LLM agents extends the same risk with more action surface.

> **2.** Capability races governance.
>
> **Q** What did MiniMax and ByteDance teach him?
>
> **A** Product shipping speed beats safety committees — Zhang left to voice concern that labs optimize benchmarks while society lacks brakes. Investors should price regulatory and reputational optionality on META/TCEHY AI rollouts.

> **3.** Distribution landlords tax the AGI feast.
>
> **Q** Where is investable exposure?
>
> **A** META and TCEHY own attention rails recommendation engines need — even if a private lab wins models, landlords capture ad and payment rent. Public investors size engagement monetization, not AGI theology.
