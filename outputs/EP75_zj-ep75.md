## Key Insights

> **1.** o1 sounds the second gold mine horn.
>
> **Q** What does Wu mean by second gold mine?
>
> **A** After scaling laws on pretrain, RL improving reasoning at inference creates new economic layer — customers pay for thinking time, not just weights. This reframes capex from training-only to train-plus-infer.

> **2.** Hidden reasoning is pricing feature.
>
> **Q** Why hide chain-of-thought?
>
> **A** Wu Yi notes safety and competition — but economically it enables metered deliberation. Investors should model reasoning tokens as ARPU driver, not cost center only.

> **3.** GOOGL must productize deep think.
>
> **Q** Where do public investors participate?
>
> **A** Gemini reasoning in Cloud, Workspace, and Search AI Overviews — GOOGL bundles into existing landlord ARPU. Failure mode is free search giving away o1-class capability.
