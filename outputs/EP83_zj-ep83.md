## Key Insights

> **1.** CUDA was a culture bet, not a forecast.
>
> **Q** Why does the book elevate CUDA?
>
> **A** Jensen funded programmable GPUs when workloads were unclear — decades of developer habit created switching costs before ChatGPT. Investors should track NIM and CUDA download curves as moat telemetry, not just datacenter revenue.

> **2.** 'Ship the whole cow' raises switching costs.
>
> **Q** What beats merchant silicon?
>
> **A** Customers buy outcomes — trained models running on integrated stacks. Bare GPU price wars hurt component vendors; Nvidia bundles networking, software, and support. $3T cap rewarded platform mix shift.

> **3.** Whiteboard culture scales truth-seeking.
>
> **Q** Why culture chapter matters for investors?
