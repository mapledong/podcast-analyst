## Key Insights

> **1.** Details compound like habits.
>
> **Q** Why does Rubin open with Wooden?
>
> **A** Legendary athletes arrived expecting drills; Wooden taught sock wrinkles cause blisters — each small habit exponential across season. Napoleon parallel: clever man neglects nothing hanging on single thread. Competition is yourself — rest is distraction; treat every word and action with skillful care.

> **2.** Void summons signal.
>
> **Q** How do Rubin, Simons, and Musk align?
>
> **A** Quiet dark room, flat, no inputs for an hour — subconscious floods when overpacked mind empties. Rubin walks, bikes, swims; news discarded for classics to hone sensitivity to greatness.
