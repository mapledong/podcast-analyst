## Key Insights

> **1.** BYD is manufacturing compounder, not developer fraud.
>
> **Q** Why reject Evergrande analogy?
>
> **A** Xieshui matches payables growth (≈6.7x) to revenue (≈6x) and units (≈9x) since 2019 — developer blowouts show payables or liabilities outpacing deliverables. BYD ships ≈4.27M cars; Evergrande stalled units.

> **2.** Compare growth rates, not scary levels.
>
> **Q** How to read ¥240B payables?
>
> **A** On ~¥780B assets (≈30%) versus 2019, ratio flat as scale grew — auto OEMs use supplier financing. Industry peer compare second; viral ~¥510B figures need gross/net context from filings.

> **3.** Price war is the real risk, not payables.
>
> **Q** What should equity investors watch?
>
> **A** Margin and supplier tolerance under Wei Jianlin scrutiny — if BYD squeezes suppliers without volume share gain, social backlash returns. BYDDY thesis lives on unit growth plus stable WC ratios, not balance-sheet scare trades.
