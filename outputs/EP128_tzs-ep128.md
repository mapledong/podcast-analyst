## Key Insights

> **1.** Next boom is edge AD silicon content.
>
> **Q** Where is chip TAM growth?
>
> **A** Experts say end-to-end driving raises TOPS per car — NVDA and 9660.HK both benefit if AD ships; difference is share per OEM geography.

> **2.** Trap is unqualified domestic win.
>
> **Q** What kills 9660.HK thesis?
>
> **A** Policy push without functional safety at scale — recalls or OEM write-offs erase design wins. Wang and Luo stress certification years, not press releases.

> **3.** OEMs dual-source by design.
>
> **Q** Can NVDA monopolize auto edge?

> **A** OEMs fear single-supplier risk — 9660.HK exists in dual-source slot even when NVDA leads performance. Investors track ASP trends under dual awards.
