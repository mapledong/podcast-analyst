## Background

> Gustav Söderström explains how Spotify thinks through major technology shifts. The smartphone forced Spotify to redesign its business model because mobile listening had been a paid feature; AI may force another rethink, but the shape is still emerging. The conversation moves from recommendation systems to generative AI, natural-language input, coding productivity, product prototyping, licensing data, and monetization. Spotify sees itself as the R&D department of the music industry, with a large user base and a history of using machine learning to make music discovery feel personal.

## Chronology

> *Spotify product evolution*
>
> **Pre-smartphone** Spotify acquired users through free desktop listening while mobile listening was a paid feature 
>
> **Smartphone era** Spotify redesigned the free mobile tier after phones threatened the desktop-led model 
>
> **ML era** Playlisting, editors, social signals, and recommendations created a personal music-friend experience 
>
> **2025** AI playlisting live in roughly 40 countries 
>
