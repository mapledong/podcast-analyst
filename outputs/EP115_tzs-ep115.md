## Key Insights

> **1.** Moat is supply chain, not discount label.
>
> **Q** What is PDD's real competitive edge?
>
> **A** Yongqing argues C2M routing and algorithmic merchant matching lower landed cost — competitors can copy UI but not ecosystem depth overnight. Investment frame tracks merchant retention and fulfillment efficiency, not GMV headlines alone.

> **2.** Temu is growth with rising friction.
>
> **Q** How should investors weight Temu?
>
> **A** Overseas GMV impresses but tariffs and local compliance raise strike price on profitability — PDD Watch requires per-market unit economics, not blended growth slides. Failure mode is buying narrative at peak Temu optimism.

> **3.** Earnings dips can be entry if moat holds.
>
> **Q** What is Curated 50 discipline here?
>
> **A** Yongqing reviews PDD after volatility — if active buyers and wallet share rise while market panics on margin, mispricing emerges. Abacus over sentiment; avoid catching falling knives if subsidy war structurally hurts take rate.
