## Key Insights

> **1.** Blogging shifted power from masthead to byline.
>
> **Q** Why did Weblogs matter?
>
> **A** Pre-2005, NYT/WSJ brand was faceless; Engadget/Gizmodo made writers the product. Jason sold Weblogs to AOL — hosts debate if independent longer would be worth more, but sale funded angel chapter. Analog blogging = photocopied Silicon Alley Reporter vs 'real' magazines. Episode context: recorded in Acquired season six (2020) with full transcript on acquired.fm; hosts Ben Gilbert and David Rosenthal apply standard playbook, grading, and investment-implications framing even for interview or Adapting-format episodes.

> **2.** Scout program was Sequoia outsourcing seed.
>
> **Q** How did Uber scout check happen?
>
> **A** Roelof recruited operator-Jason ≈2006 with 50/50 carry split on scout investments — not Jason's money. Travis/Uber among first; Sequoia later uncertain on repeating scouts but Jason had Stripe, Thumbtack, Datastax exposure. Irony: Jason puzzled why LPs back scouts who don't meet companies — same structure Sequoia used with him.

> **3.** Poker informs portfolio construction.
>
> **Q** How many angel bets are enough?
>
> **A** Treat small checks as experiments; asymmetric upside requires volume. Non-pro investors should prefer post-revenue; Jason can do Calm-style pre-revenue at his skill level. 'Consensus kills' at seed — need weird deals others pass. Failed experiments mental frame vs loss aversion. Hosts tie lesson to Acquired playbook themes for builders and investors reviewing the episode.

> **4.** Rejected VC partnership deliberately.
>
> **Q** Why not join Sequoia?
>
> **A** Wanted solo artist not band — understood returns to scale but chose TWiST + Syndicate + LAUNCH to scale without carry split across partnership. Mahalo post-Panda: could have gone VC; doubled down on personal platform instead.

> **5.** Deconstructing VC job functions.
>
> **Q** What is Jason building toward?
>
> **A** LP-show tease: unbundle sourcing (show), evaluation, winning deals, helping founders, future capital — AngelList syndicates as mechanical layer. Relationship 'hustle' is moat: Mark Cuban Weblogs era, Travis poker dynamic, CNBC visibility drives paid views.
