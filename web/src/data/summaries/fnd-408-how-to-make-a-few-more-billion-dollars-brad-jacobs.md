## Key Insights

> **1.** Capital source shapes freedom.
>
> **Q** Why does Jacobs favor public markets?
>
> **A** Non-dilutive access without board seats or coupons; quarterly earnings discipline; retail and index investors as marketing. Family offices offer operator wisdom; pension money is sticky; hedge funds flip — he warns on misalignment.

> **2.** Integration speed beats elegance.
>
> **Q** When does M&A integration start?
>
> **A** Before close with unrestricted access — town halls within hours, Q&A not lectures, intelligence dashboards for all levels. Band-aid rip on legacy tech and branding; lingering under old structures limits upside.

> **3.** Mindset is operational infrastructure.
>
> **Q** How does Jacobs handle perfectionism?
>
> **A** TM since 16, twice-daily meditation, Ellis REBT demanding flexible preferences not musts — 'Okay, how upset were you?' Apollo Ohno noted Jacobs as rare positively fueled founder among eight billion-dollar outcomes.
