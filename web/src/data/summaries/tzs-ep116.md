## Key Facts

> **F1** Growth framework: Yongqing ranks moat durability, reinvestment ROI, and entry multiple — NVDA Watch when data-center growth and margin justify capex boom; not every AI name deserves NVDA multiple.

> **F2** Landlord versus volume: GOOGL distributes AI across Search, Cloud, YouTube — better public risk-reward than pre-revenue agents. Pair with NVDA as compute plus distribution barbell.

> **F3** Position sizing: Curated 50 discipline — add on mispricing when thesis intact, trim at narrative peak. High-growth tech requires explicit sell rules when growth decelerates or competition erodes margin.

## Key Insights

> **1.** Not every AI stock is NVDA.
>
> **Q** What is Yongqing's core warning?
>
> **A** 25 years taught him feast narratives overpay mediocrities — apply NVDA/GOOGL checklist to any high-growth name: moat, reinvestment, multiple.

> **2.** GOOGL is public AI barbell leg.
>
> **Q** Why pair with NVDA in framework?
>
> **A** NVDA is compute feast; GOOGL is distribution landlord — together cover AI stack with liquid large caps. Avoid double-counting same theme in oversized positions.

> **3.** Sell rules matter in growth.
>
> **Q** What differs from value investing?
>
> **A** Yongqing trims when growth decelerates or competition hits margin even if story sounds intact — high-growth requires faster thesis kill switches than baijiu compounders.
