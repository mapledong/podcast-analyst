## Background

> Michael Ovitz uses stories from art, entertainment, finance, and technology to explain how he spots talent early and helps turn individual potential into durable institutions. He discusses MoMA's Glenn Lowry, Alex Karp at Palantir, Steve Schwarzman at Blackstone, Mark and Ben at Andreessen Horowitz, Josh Kushner at Thrive, and Larry Gagosian. The discussion is practical and personal: boards that meet monthly, leaders who go first, trust-based dealmaking, time as a scarce input, and how CAA became a strategic force in entertainment rather than a simple talent agency.
