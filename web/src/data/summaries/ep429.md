## Background

> Jay Hoag co-founded TCV around 1995 and has invested through 30 years of technology cycles. TCV is a growth investor behind companies like Netflix, Spotify, Expedia, Revolut, and others. The conversation compares 1990s venture scale with today's trillion-dollar technology market, explains TCV's sourcing machine, and reflects on holding great companies through public-market pain.

> **2.** Every great company goes through investor disillusionment.
>
> **Q** What is the most common growth-investing mistake?
>
