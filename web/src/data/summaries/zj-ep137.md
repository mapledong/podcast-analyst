## Background

> In a 264-minute episode, Zhang Xiaojun speaks with Hong Letong on AI for mathematics. Action, her company, closed a $1.6 billion Series A to industrialize Lean formal proof workflows: researchers draft an outline, formalize statements in Lean, then complete proofs or mark sorries. She contrasts free attention (models wandering) with bounded attention (structured proof steps) — math as structural language, not calculation tricks. Her 2020 paper on graphs, catching, and proof anchors the technical path. B Capital participated in fundraising; an Ohio State professor left academia to join. The thesis: formal verification unlocks trustworthy AI reasoning in science and code, not just benchmark math olympiads.
