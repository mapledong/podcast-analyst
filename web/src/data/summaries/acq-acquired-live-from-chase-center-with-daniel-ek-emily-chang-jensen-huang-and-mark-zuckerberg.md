>
> **Q** Did Google make money on YouTube?

>

> **A** Streaming ≈$100M+/year from Spotify alone in 2023; Eras tour ≈$1.1B gross 2023; David estimates ≈$550M+ annual FCF versus Forbes ≈$1.1B net worth. Emily Chang segment debates whether Disney 20x FCF multiple applies to single-artist IP — Taylor Swift Inc as cash-flow machine independent of label economics.
